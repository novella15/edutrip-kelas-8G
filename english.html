Ini adalah halaman English

Dalam pelajaran ini, saya membuat sebuah mindmap yang bisa membantu saya membuat hasil akhir Travel Writing selama edutrip di Taiwan. Mindmap ini terisi dari pertanyaan-pertanyaan yang harus dijawab saat kunjungi ke tempat yang kami ingin tulis untuk bisa mengingat kembali apa yang kami lakukan.

Pertanyaan:

  1. Is it worth it to try?
  2. How do they cook the food?
  3. What will I feel after eating the food?
  4. Who are the people I am going to meet?
  5. What will be the color of the stalls?
  6. Why is this place popular?
  7. What language are they going to talk to us?
  8. How is this night market different from other night markets?
  9. Will there be any Indonesia food?
  10. What types of food will be there?

Jawaban:

  1. Yes, the food is sweet and the area is clean.
  2. They make the dough of the milk ball, then they fry it until it's crispy. After that, they coat it with lots of sugar and it is ready to be served.
  3. I feel my sweet tooth jumping in happiness from the sweetness and the crispiness of the milk balls.
  4. I met a lot of Taiwanese people, but I also saw people from Japan and Korea roaming around the night market.
  5. Most of the stalls are white, black, and grey.
  6. This night market was built near a univerity wear students could come here and eat after studying. Because of that, this place is packed with university students. Other than that, this place is also famous for its view and food which also attracts tourist around the world.
  7. The language I talked to them is mostly chinese mized with english.
  8. This night market is different from others as it is near a university and it is known to be one of the largest night markets.
  9. Sadly, there was no Indonesian food around.
  10. Types of food that are found are mostly chinese food and taiwanese food.
